Recipe: junior s lemon coconut cheesecake
Minutes: 255
Tags: cheesecake, course, desserts
Ingredients: angel flake coconut, baking powder, cake flour, cold water, cornstarch, cream cheese, cream of tartar, extra-large egg yolks, extra large eggs, fresh lemon juice, heavy cream, lemon extract, lemon rind, pure vanilla extract, salt
Steps:
01. preheat the oven to 350 f and generously butter a 9-inch springform pan
02. sift the cake flour , baking powder , and salt together in a medium-sized bowl and set aside
03. beat the egg yolks together in a large bowl with an electric mixer on high for 3 minutes
04. then , with the mixer still running , gradually add the 1 / 3 cup of sugar and continue beating until thick light-yellow ribbons form in the bowl , about 5 minutes more
05. beat in the vanilla and lemon extracts
06. sift the flour mixture over the batter and stir it in by hand until no more white flecks appear
07. then blend in the butter
08. in a clean bowl , using clean dry beaters , beat the egg whites and cream of tartar together on high until frothy
