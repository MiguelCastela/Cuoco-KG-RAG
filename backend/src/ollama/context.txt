Recipe: divine boneless duck breast with port wine sauce
Minutes: 540
Tags: course, dinner-party, duck, duck-breasts, main-dish, main-ingredient, meat, occasion, poultry
Ingredients: balsamic vinegar, beef broth, boneless duck breasts, dry red wine, fresh ginger, garlic clove, garlic cloves, heavy cream, lemon juice, olive oil, shallot, soy sauce, sugar, tawny port, water
Steps:
01. marinade:
02. in a bowl whisk together the wine , the vinegar , soy sauce , lemon juice , garlic , gingerroot , oil and salt and pepper to taste
03. put the duck breasts in a large resealable plastic bag , pour the marinade over them , and seal the bag
04. put the plastic bag in a large bowl and let the duck marinate , chilled , overnight
05. remove the duck from the marinade and pat it dry between layers of paper towels
06. preheat oven to 450
07. score the skin of each duck breast in a crosshatch pattern with a sharp knife and sprinkle both sides of the duck with salt and pepper to taste
08. heat 2 heavy skillets over moderately high heat until they are hot and in each skillet cook 1 of the duck breasts , skin side down , for 10 minutes
